Our Beginning

How KAAPI Came to Life

In 2012, five coffee enthusiasts from different corners of Kerala came together with a shared vision - to bring authentic Malabar coffee culture to the modern world while preserving traditional brewing methods. What started as late-night conversations in a Kozhikode coffee shop blossomed into KAAPI, now serving over 10,000 cups daily across Kerala.

The Visionaries

Meet Our Founding Team

Arya Siddarth

Coffee Expert & Innovation Head

Flavor alchemist who creates our seasonal specials. His Jackfruit Cold Brew won "Most Innovative Coffee" at the 2020 India Coffee Awards.

Thanmai Bhat

Head of Supplies

Former agricultural scientist who established direct trade relationships with 42 small coffee growers across the Western Ghats, ensuring fair prices and organic practices.

Monish Malpe

Master Roaster & CEO

Fourth-generation coffee roaster from Chikmagalur who brought his family's wood-fired roasting techniques to KAAPI. His blends have won three Golden Bean awards.

Abhishek Holla

Culinary Director

Trained in Paris but passionate about Kerala cuisine. Created our famous "Pazham Pori Kaapi" pairing that's been featured in Food & Wine magazine.

Our Founders' Philosophy
