About Us

Brewing Happiness Since 1947

Our Story

From the misty hills of Wayanad to your cup

KAAPI began as a small family-run stall in Kasargod, where our grandfather would roast coffee beans over wood fire and brew them in traditional brass filters. The aroma would draw people from neighboring villages, creating a community around our humble coffee. Today, while we've grown, we still follow his exact recipes and techniques, bringing you authentic Kerala coffee just as nature intended.

Our Vision

To preserve and share the rich coffee heritage of Kerala while supporting sustainable farming practices. We work directly with small coffee growers in the Western Ghats, ensuring fair prices and organic cultivation methods that protect our land for future generations.
