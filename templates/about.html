About Us

Our Heritage

Brewing Traditions Since 1968

From Kerala's Hills to Your Cup

What began as a humble coffee stall in Thrissur's famous temple festival has grown into Kerala's most cherished coffee experience. Founded by the late Gopalan Nair, a third-generation coffee planter, KAAPI preserves the authentic flavors of Malabar coffee through traditional methods.

Single-Estate Beans

Sourced from 17 family-owned estates in Wayanad's highlands

Wood-Fired Roasting

Using aromatic jackfruit wood for that signature smoky flavor

Direct Trade

Ensuring fair prices for our partner farmers since 1972

Cultural Legacy

Preserving Kerala's unique coffee house traditions

Our Craft

The Art of Perfect Kaapi

Shade-Grown

Our coffee grows slowly under pepper vines and banana trees, developing complex flavors from Kerala's volcanic soil.

Traditional Roasting

Small batches roasted over mango wood fires, a technique perfected over generations.

Brass Filter Brew

Our signature decoction comes from handcrafted brass filters that create the perfect extraction.

Perfect Pour

Served in traditional steel tumblers, poured from height to create the signature froth.
